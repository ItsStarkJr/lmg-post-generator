Prison Break (Kouchoku) with example

by Wessel Strijkstra

I made this puzzle for the lovely Wisty, as a thank you for her nice donation to the Sudokucon live stream. I think it turned out quite fun:) I wouldn't necessarily recommend solving it if you have no experience with the genre, but who am I to not recommend a Kouch. If you do want some practice beforehand, you can find a couple of them on puzz.link. Have fun!

Rules:

  • Draw lines connecting all clues to form a loop which travels through each clue once.
  • Lines only travel straight from clue to clue (allowing non-orthogonal lines).
  • Two line segments may intersect each other only at a 90Â° angle.
  • All clues containing the same letter must be connected consecutively.
  • Clues containing different letters may not be directly connected; the loop must travel through at least one black dot between them.
